About NatureNotes

Getting outdoors doesn't have to be a chore!

NatureNotes gives you a way to keep track of and save all your favorite camping spots, along with trails near those campsites and amenities offered at each site.

That way, you can get outside on a whim and revisit your favorite places over and over without all the head-scratching of trying to remember where it was that you liked!
