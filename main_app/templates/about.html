About NatureNotes

Getting outdoors doesn't have to be a chore!

NatureNotes is an app designed to make your camping experience more enjoyable and organized.

Whether you're an avid camper or just starting out, NatureNotes offers a user-friendly platform to create and manage your personalized list of campsites completed with amenities and nearby hiking trails.
